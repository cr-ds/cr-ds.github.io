When are the most played Clash Royale games?

Battles per Hour a Day

In order to determine the match times, we statistically analyzed all time stamps of the matches from March 1st to 18th. After grouping by day, converting to the German time zone and adjusting the data, the result was as follows. Many of the highest match times took place during the week and between 9 am and 11 pm.

Win Rate per Hour per Day

The question was continued by analyzing whether there is a correlation between the time of day and the number of matches won. Therefore, we summarized all weekdays of our data set and summarized the win rate per hour and day. So if you play Clash Royale and don't want to lose against top players, avoid Mondays and Sundays. Play on Thursdays.
